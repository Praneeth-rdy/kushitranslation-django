ABOUT US

Kushi Translations & Allied Services

According to the analysis of the recent census, there are 121 languages which are spoken by 10,000 or more people in India. A plethora of individuals struggle tocommunicate as they navigate their everyday lives. In the wake of globalization and rapid migrations, boundaries might have disappeared but not language barriers. Right from your birth certificates to complex legal documents, we often struggle to decode the true intent of such texts.  Just as a lack of understanding can be disconcerting, the experience of being understood is empowering. Empower yourself with our language solutions where language barriers no longer exist. Expand your reach & limits with our affordable translations and other allied services. With our dedicated team of translators and domain experts, we deliver quality services tailored to your needs.
